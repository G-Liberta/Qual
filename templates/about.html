About Qual

This is a web application designed to provide quizzes for users to test their knowledge in various subjects. QuAL offers a wide range of questions and topics to cater to different interests and educational needs. Our goal is to make learning enjoyable and engaging through interactive quizzes.

Meet the Minds Behind Qual

QUAL is the brainchild of Arber Dresha and Liberta Gani, two passionate quality assurance (QA) professionals who have a combined track record of excellence in the software development industry. Their shared experience working on numerous projects together has fostered a strong working relationship and a deep understanding of the importance of robust software testing.

Arber Dresha

Arber brings a wealth of experience to the table, having honed his skills in various QA methodologies across diverse software domains. His meticulous attention to detail ensures that even the most intricate bugs are identified and addressed. Arber's expertise lies in:

  • **Automation Testing:** Streamlining the testing process through the creation and execution of automated test scripts.
  • **Performance Testing:** Identifying bottlenecks and optimizing software performance for a seamless user experience.
  • **Security Testing:** Safeguarding applications from potential vulnerabilities with a keen eye for security risks.

Liberta Gani

Liberta's background in QA is marked by a user-centric approach. She champions the importance of testing from the user's perspective, ensuring that the final product is intuitive and user-friendly. Her areas of expertise include:

  • **Functional Testing:** Verifying application functionality aligns with design specifications.
  • **Usability Testing:** Evaluating user experience and identifying potential usability issues.
  • **Regression Testing:** Re-running crucial tests after updates to ensure existing functionalities remain intact.
